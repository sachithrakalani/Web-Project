Pizza

Pizza is an Italian meal that consists of a normally circular, flat foundation of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, mozzarella, and sometimes other ingredients, and then cooked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A pizzetta is another name for a little pizza.
