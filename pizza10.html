Thai Chicken Pizza


Ingredients

  1. 1 prebaked 12-inch pizza crust
  2. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  3. 4 ounces ready-to-serve roasted chicken breast strips
  4. 2 to 3 large fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
  5. 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons Thai peanut sauce, divided
  6. 2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese, divided
  7. 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  8. 1/2 cup fresh snow peas, halved
  9. 1/4 cup chopped sweet red pepper
  10. 1/4 cup julienned carrot
  11. 2 green onions, sliced
  12. 1/2 cup salted peanuts

Procedure

  • STEP 1
    Preheat oven to 450Â°. Place crust on an ungreased 12-in. pizza pan; brush with oil. In a small bowl, combine the chicken, mushrooms and 1/4 cup peanut sauce; spread over crust. Sprinkle with 1 cup cheese.


  • STEP 2
    Layer with the red onion, snow peas, red pepper and carrot. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Top with green onions and peanuts. Drizzle with remaining peanut sauce.


  • STEP 3
    Bake until cheese is melted, 10-12 minutes.
