Seafood Pizza


Ingredients

  1. 2 package Refrigerated crescent rolls
  2. 1/2 cup bennet's Chili, Cocktail or Hot Seafood Sauce
  3. 1/4 lb Peeled cooked small shrimp
  4. 1 cn orleans Shrimp, drained and soaked as label directs (4 1/3 oz)
  5. 1/4 lb Imitation crab blend, flaked
  6. 1 cn harris or orleans Crab Meat, drained
  7. 1/2 cup Sliced green onions
  8. 1/2 cup Chopped green bell pepper
  9. 1/2 cup Chopped red bell pepper

Procedure

  • STEP 1
    Preheat oven to 400'F. Unroll crescent roll dough; press on bottom of x10" baking sheet, pressing perforations together. Bake 10 minutes or until golden. Cool. Combine sour cream and sauce; spread over crust. Top with remaining ingredients. Chill. Cut into squares to serve. Refrigerate leftovers. Makes 1 15x10" pie.
